Getting Started

Start With Prayer

Life change is an impossible task without God working in our lives. Fortunately, the scripture presents life transformation as a foregone conclusion in the scope of God's desire for us.

Begin this assessment by presenting yourself humbly dependant upon Him. Ask God to help you see your life clearly as you respond to each statement.

Assessing Your Life

The following 120 statements relate to the core beliefs, practices, and virtues of the developing disciple. Read each statement and assess your personal Christian experience on a scale of 0 to 5:

Does not apply at all → 0   1   2   3   4   5 → Applies completely

What You'll Receive

After completing all 120 statements, you'll receive a detailed profile showing your scores across six dimensions — Beliefs, Practices, and Virtues for both Love God and Love Neighbor — along with a visual bar graph of each area.
